Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

newleaf is a browser extension that replaces your new tab page with a clean dashboard showing the date, time, a search box, and your favorite links. This policy explains what data newleaf handles and what it does not.

The short version: newleaf has no servers and no accounts, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything you configure stays in your own browser. A few features rely on standard Google web requests, which are described below so you know exactly what leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. newleaf has no backend, no analytics, no tracking, no cookies set by us, and no user accounts. We never see your data, because it never reaches us.

Data stored on your device

The following is saved locally in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device or gets sent to us:

  • Your quick links — the labels and URLs you add.
  • Your settings — such as 12/24-hour time, date format, and link ordering.

This data lives only in the browser profile where you use newleaf. You can erase all of it at any time by removing the extension or clearing your browser's site data for the new tab page.

Third-party requests

newleaf itself does not transmit your data, but a few features cause your browser to contact Google directly. These are standard web requests, and the data involved is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Search. When you use the search box, your browser submits your query to Google Search (google.com/search), exactly as if you typed it into Google yourself. newleaf does not see, log, or store your searches.
  • Link icons (favicons). To show an icon next to each saved link, your browser requests it from Google's favicon service (google.com/s2/favicons). This request includes the hostname of the saved link (for example, example.com) and your IP address. The full URL, link label, and the rest of your link list are never sent.
  • Web fonts. newleaf loads the "Outfit" typeface from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). As with any web font, this request includes your IP address and user-agent.

newleaf does not use any other third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers. The background photos are bundled inside the extension and are not fetched from any external source.

Permissions

newleaf requests no browser permissions. It only overrides the new tab page.

Children's privacy

newleaf does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at the same location.
